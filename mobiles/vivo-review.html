    The Vivo T2 5G smartphone has an FHD+ AMOLED display for easy access and immersive viewing. The 90 Hz AMOLED display allows you to multitask easily. The FlashCharge enables you with fast charging and determines the optimal charging strategy if the phone is in use or if the screen is off while plugged in. This smartphone allows you to use multiple apps at the same time, thanks to its Extended RAM 3.0. The Bokeh Flare Portrait makes you stand out in the crowd, so that you can take stunning photos.
